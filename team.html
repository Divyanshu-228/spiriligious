Our People

Built by Believers

Spiriligious is not just a platform — it is a calling. It is built by people who believe that spirituality, research, and technology should work together to illuminate the path of wisdom. Our team brings together diverse expertise, united by a shared reverence for India's spiritual heritage and a commitment to presenting it with clarity, integrity, and depth.

Founder & Visionary

Guiding the Way

Dr. (Lt.) Hemant Kumar Joshi

Founder

A visionary with a deep passion for India's spiritual heritage, Dr. Joshi has dedicated his life to bridging the gap between ancient traditions and modern understanding. With a background in both scientific research and spiritual studies, he founded Spiriligious to create a space where faith and reason coexist harmoniously. His work focuses on extracting the timeless wisdom embedded in our scriptures and presenting it in a way that is both authentic and accessible to the modern seeker.

Our traditions are not merely rituals — they are a living science of the soul. Spiriligious is my humble attempt to rekindle that flame.
