FAQ

Common Questions

How can I contribute research or articles to Spiriligious?

Send your research, articles, or ideas to research@spiriligious.in. Our editorial team reviews all submissions and will get back to you within 5–7 working days. We welcome well-researched, authentic content that aligns with our mission.

I want to volunteer. How do I get involved?

Wonderful! Please fill in the contact form with "Volunteer" as the category, or email us at info@spiriligious.in. Tell us about your skills, interests, and how much time you can offer. We'll find the right role for you.

What kind of content does Spiriligious accept?

We accept well-researched articles on saints, temples, spiritual traditions, scientific analysis of rituals, encyclopedia entries, and original research. All content must be authentic, respectful, and grounded in credible sources.

I found an error or technical issue. How do I report it?

Please report any errors or technical issues to support@spiriligious.in or use the "Technical Issue" category in our contact form. We appreciate your help in making Spiriligious better for everyone.
