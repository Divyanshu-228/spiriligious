Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Spiriligious ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require any personal information to browse our content. However, we may collect:

  • Voluntary submissions: When you contact us via email or fill a contact form, we receive your name and email address.
  • Usage data: We may use anonymous analytics (e.g., page views) to understand which articles are popular. We do not use tracking cookies that identify you personally.
  • YouTube embeds: Our video pages include embedded YouTube players. YouTube may set its own cookies and collect data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your queries or feedback.
  • To improve our content and website experience.
  • To send occasional updates about new bhajans or articles (only if you explicitly subscribe).

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. Third‑Party Links

Our website contains links to YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and X (Twitter). These sites have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information you provide. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our content is intended for general audiences and not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this page occasionally. The "Last updated" date will reflect changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at privacy@spiriligious.com (or use the Contact page).
