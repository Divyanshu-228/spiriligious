About Spiriligious

Spiriligious is a platform that bridges ancient traditions with modern science. We believe every ritual, belief, and practice has a logical and scientific foundation that has been obscured over time.

Our mission is to revive the spirit of inquiry – to question, analyze, and discover the truth behind traditions, while respecting their spiritual essence.

We are a team of researchers, scientists, and spiritual seekers dedicated to balanced churning (मंथन) of knowledge.

Join us in this journey of ज्ञान, अध्यात्म और विज्ञान – exploring the unknown, questioning the known, and celebrating the eternal wisdom of our heritage.
