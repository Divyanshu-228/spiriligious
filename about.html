Our Journey

The Story Behind Spiriligious

Spiriligious was born from a simple yet profound realization: ancient traditions are not mere rituals — they are encrypted wisdom. For centuries, India's spiritual heritage has been passed down through generations, often misunderstood as superstition rather than the sophisticated science it truly is.

Our founder, Dr. (Lt.) Hemant Kumar Joshi, envisioned a platform where faith meets reason and where every mantra, every temple, and every tradition is examined with the reverence it deserves — not as blind belief, but as a living wisdom tradition.

Today, Spiriligious stands as a bridge between the ancient and the modern, the spiritual and the scientific, the personal and the universal. We are a community of seekers, researchers, and believers united by a shared purpose: to churn the ocean of knowledge and discover the nectar within.
